GENEA Initiative: The GENEA (Generation and Evaluation of Non-verbal Agent behavior) Initiative advances research in generating and evaluating human motion and non-verbal behavior for virtual agents. Through annual workshops, challenges, and leaderboards, GENEA fosters collaboration and benchmarking in the community.
